Klaus Nielsen

About the project

Klaus Nielsen is the founder of a successful tech enterprise, a monk, and an international spiritual teacher. Klaus fuses ancient wisdom with modern business strategies to offer a superlative coaching model tailored to founders and executives.

He has a simple website where one can find more information about him and the benefits of his coaching and sessions. He wasn’t satisfied with the color palette and the general look of his website. Since his target audience consists predominantly of CEOs and company founders the goal was to change the design of his website to have a more modern and proffesioal look.

The website was designed in Adobe XD and was developed in Squarespace.
