Blue Lotus

About the project

Blue Lotus is a spa salon that offers various services and treatments for body and faces care, aesthetic medicine, and more in a beautiful and relaxing environment. The task was to create a visually appealing website that will be able to convey the relaxing and pleasant atmosphere of a spa salon and show its unique features.

The website was designed in Adobe XD and was developed, and made responsive using HTML, CSS, JavaScript language, and GSAP animation library.
