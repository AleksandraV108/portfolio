About

Although I graduated from Economics and Business school, being surrounded by many artists, designers, and spiritualists I naturally gravitated toward creativity seeing it as a way to connect with others. I loved the feeling of creating something and design was just a hobby for me till I finally got the opportunity in the spring of 2020 to enroll at IT Academy, an international school for the most sought-after IT professions. I chose design and multimedia- web design area. There I got the most advanced knowledge of HTML, and CSS, a basic understanding of JavaScript, and the use of software like Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe XD for creating visual elements.

For two years I participated in ITAcademy Design Studio where students are given an opportunity to work on real projects like logo design, product label design, websites, and many other great projects and gain great experience under the supervision of professionals.

Today I am happy that I can do what I really love and mix all of my knowledge and experience to provide work across design creating elegant, simple and creative visual identities that can help you to connect better with your customers.

Outside work I love photography, music, digital art, and fashion design.
