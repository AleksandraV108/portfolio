Essential Healing

About the project

Maja Vrieling (Mangala) is an independent doTERRA Consultant, who works actively with essential oils. Through her website, She shares her experience and knowledge about therapeutic-grade essential oils' healing powers and offers training in aromatherapy, energy healing, or meditation.

The main problem with her previous website was an outdated and not attractive enough design that was not presenting her in the right way. To solve this, the focus was to create a website that has simplicity, clarity, harmony, elegance, and energy by adding attractive images, changing the font, color palette, and layout to a more clean and modern one so that the user doesn’t have trouble identifying the information they need.

The website was designed in Adobe XD and was developed in WordPress.
